Receipe :

Biryani :-

	- 1 and 1/2 cup Organic Basmati Rice
	- soaked for half an hour
	- 1 and 1/2 cup Potato cut into 1/2 inch cubes
	- 3/4 cup Carrots cut into 1/2 inch cubes
	- 7/8 French beans in 1/2 inch pieces
	- 1/2cup Cauliflower cut into medium size florets
	- 4/5 strands saffron soaked in 2 tbsp Milk
	- 3 large Onions thinly sliced and Fried
	- 3 tbsp Churned Curd
	- 4/5tsp Organic Biryani Masala
	- 4 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
	- 5 tbsp Ghee
	- 6 Halved Cashews
	- 10-12 Raisins
	- Salt to Taste
